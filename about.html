About Booth

Booth is one of the biggest showpieces of Carnegie Mellon's Spring Carnival! Student organizations build multi-story structures around our chosen theme. These booths include interactive games and elaborate decorations. The booths are located on Midway, at the College of Fine Arts parking lot. Admission is free to see booths!
